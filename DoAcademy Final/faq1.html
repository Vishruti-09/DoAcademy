    What kinds of books do you buy?

    We buy mostly college textbooks, but sometimes other books as well. Most of the books we purchase were published within the last 3 years. What we buy can change day by day or week by week depending on resale values, our current inventory, demand, and other factors.

    Are there any fees for selling my books?

    No. Our service is free to use; there are no hidden fees. We offer free price quotes, free shipping, and fast payment. Give us a try today!

    Should I create a separate order (seller cart) for each box of books that I am selling?

    No, please create one order. If you need multiple boxes to ship your order, please be sure to include a package slip with your Order ID inside each package. This will make things run more smoothly once your order arrives in our receiving department.

    How do I get paid?

    We will mail you a check, or pay you via PayPal.
